My military service

My military service involved serving in the role of "Mashakit Tash" from November 2019 to November 2021. As a Mashakit Tash, my responsibility was to ensure the living conditions of soldiers. Throughout my service, I developed social skills and discovered a high level of emotional intelligence. I was tasked with ensuring the basic living conditions of all soldiers in the Coastal District of the Israel Police. This included conducting home visits, engaging in telephone conversations with soldiers' families and commanders, listening to personal stories, and providing a sympathetic ear.
